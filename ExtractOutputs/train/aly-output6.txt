Some people use 5 ingredients and some include more than 10.  It really depends on the family. And that is what makes Futomaki such fun sushi to share!</p> <p>The most common ingredients include cucumber, seasoned kanpyo (gourd strips), shiitake mushrooms, and tamagoyaki (Japanese rolled omelette).</p> <p>In my rolls, I include the following 7 ingredients:</p> <ol> <li>Tamagoyaki / Dashimaki Tamago</li> <li>Simmered Kanpyo (gourd strips)</li> <li>Simmered Shiitake Mushrooms</li> <li>Mitsuba or Spinach</li> <li>Cucumber</li> <li>Cooked unagi (freshwater eel) or anago (saltwater eel)</li> <li>Sakura Denbu (Seasoned Codfish Flakes)</li> </ol> <p>Other ingredients include <em>kamaboko</em> fish cakes (or imitation crab), <a href="https://www.justonecookbook.com/koyadofu/"><em>koyadofu</em></a> (freeze-dry tofu), shiso leaves, pickled ginger, carrots, tuna (sashimi), and salmon (sashimi).</p> <p>I understand some of these ingredients are hard to find in your local Asian grocery stores. You will have better luck finding the products in Japanese grocery stores.</p> <p>But remember, you can make futomaki with anything that you really like! <em>futomaki</em> all year round, except for one day out of the whole year.</p> <p>On February 3rd, the day of <a href="https://www.justonecookbook.com/setsubun/" rel="noopener noreferrer" title="Setsubun">Setsubun</a> (節分, the day before the beginning of spring in Japan), the same sushi roll is called <i><strong>ehō-maki</strong> </i>(恵方巻).</p> <h2><strong>What is Ehomaki?</strong></h2> <p><i><strong>Ehō-maki</strong> </i>(恵方巻) is eaten <span style="text-decoration:underline;">uncut</span>, like a burrito, and you would eat it while facing the lucky direction (the eho).  This lucky direction changes every year depending on the zodiac symbol. For 2020, the lucky direction is West-southwest.</p> <p>On the evening of Setsubun, we have a custom of throwing roasted soybeans around the house, and outside from the window while shouting “Oni wa soto!  Fuku wa uchi!” (“Devils out, happiness in”).</p> <figure aria-describedby="caption-attachment-92547" class="wp-caption aligncenter" id="attachment_92547" style="width:800px;"><img alt="" class="wp-image-92547 size-full nitro-lazy nitro-lazy-empty" data-pin-title="Futomaki 太巻き (Maki Sushi)" height="533" id="MTU4MjM4OTgwMC41MjMz" nitro-lazy-src="https://d3eh3svpl1busq.cloudfront.net/KQYMGOLIdXGmoAcyJsPOrQDKktgCbwtG/assets/static/optimized/rev-c031098/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Setsubun-I-2.jpg" nitro-lazy-srcset="https://d3eh3svpl1busq.cloudfront.net/KQYMGOLIdXGmoAcyJsPOrQDKktgCbwtG/assets/static/optimized/rev-c031098/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Setsubun-I-2.jpg 800w, https://d3eh3svpl1busq.cloudfront.net/KQYMGOLIdXGmoAcyJsPOrQDKktgCbwtG/assets/static/optimized/rev-c031098/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Setsubun-I-2-300x200.jpg 300w, https://d3eh3svpl1busq.cloudfront.net/KQYMGOLIdXGmoAcyJsPOrQDKktgCbwtG/assets/static/optimized/rev-c031098/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Setsubun-I-2-768x512.jpg 768w, https://d3eh3svpl1busq.cloudfront.net/KQYMGOLIdXGmoAcyJsPOrQDKktgCbwtG/assets/static/optimized/rev-c031098/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Setsubun-I-2-600x400.jpg 600w" nitro-og-height="533" nitro-og-width="800" sizes="(max-width: 800px) 100vw, 800px" width="800"/><figcaption class="wp-caption-text" id="caption-attachment-92547">Roasted soybeans, Okame &amp;
