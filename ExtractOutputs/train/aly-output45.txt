<p>My mom left for Japan after visiting for 2 weeks.  While she was here, we went to Monterey/Carmel the first weekend and Seattle the 2nd weekend.  During the weekdays, we spent a lot of time at parks where my son proudly rode his bicycle (ahem, still with training wheels) and my daughter showed off her monkey bar skills.  My mom also saw her granddaughter’s confident face during her very first swimming lesson, and at the same time she also learned her grandson often gets into trouble during swim lessons because he never pays attention to the teacher.  I’m glad she got to see some of our daily life and enjoyed her stay with us.</p>

<div class="wprm-recipe-ingredient-group">
<ul class="wprm-recipe-ingredients">
<li class="wprm-recipe-ingredient">
<span class="wprm-recipe-ingredient-amount">10</span>
<span class="wprm-recipe-ingredient-name">Brussels Sprouts</span>
</li>
<li class="wprm-recipe-ingredient">
<span class="wprm-recipe-ingredient-amount">1</span>
<span class="wprm-recipe-ingredient-unit">Tbsp</span>
<span class="wprm-recipe-ingredient-name">extra virgin olive oil</span>
</li>
<li class="wprm-recipe-ingredient">
<span class="wprm-recipe-ingredient-amount">2</span>
<span class="wprm-recipe-ingredient-unit">slices</span>
<span class="wprm-recipe-ingredient-name">applewood smoked bacon</span>
</li>
<li class="wprm-recipe-ingredient">
<span class="wprm-recipe-ingredient-amount">2-3</span>
<span class="wprm-recipe-ingredient-unit">strings</span>
<span class="wprm-recipe-ingredient-name">thymes</span>
<span class="wprm-recipe-ingredient-notes">(fresh)</span>

<span class="wprm-recipe-ingredient-amount">2</span>
<span class="wprm-recipe-ingredient-unit">tsp</span>
<span class="wprm-recipe-ingredient-name">apple cider vinegar</span>
</li>
<li class="wprm-recipe-ingredient">
<span class="wprm-recipe-ingredient-unit">Small pinch</span>
<span class="wprm-recipe-ingredient-name">kosher/sea salt (use half for table salt)</span>

<div class="wprm-recipe-instructions-container">
<div class="wprm-recipe-header wprm-color-header">Instructions</div>
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-group">
<ol class="wprm-recipe-instructions">
<li class="wprm-recipe-instruction">
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-text"><p>Gather all the ingredients.</p><p>Bring a large pot of water to a boil and salt it generously.</p></div>
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-image"><img alt="Brussels Sprouts with Bacon and Thyme 1" class="attachment-700 size-700" height="233" sizes="(max-width: 350px) 100vw, 350px" src="https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-1-e1312148853193.jpg" srcset="https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-1-e1312148853193.jpg 350w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-1-e1312148853193-300x200.jpg 300w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-1-e1312148853193-205x135.jpg 205w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-1-e1312148853193-259x173.jpg 259w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-1-e1312148853193-150x100.jpg 150w" width="350"/></div>
</li>
<li class="wprm-recipe-instruction">
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-text">Trim the bottom of Brussel sprouts, leaving the core intact, and cut in half. Cut bacon crosswise into ½ -inch strips.<br/></div>
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-image"><img alt="Brussels Sprouts with Bacon and Thyme 2" class="attachment-700 size-700" height="233" sizes="(max-width: 350px) 100vw, 350px" src="https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-2-e1312149176769.jpg" srcset="https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-2-e1312149176769.jpg 350w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-2-e1312149176769-300x200.jpg 300w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-2-e1312149176769-205x135.jpg 205w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-2-e1312149176769-259x173.jpg 259w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-2-e1312149176769-150x100.jpg 150w" width="350"/></div>
</li>
<li class="wprm-recipe-instruction">
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-text">When the water boils, throw in the Brussel sprouts and cook uncovered for 6 minutes, until crisp-tender. Drain in a colander and set aside.<br/></div>
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-image"><img alt="Brussels Sprouts with Bacon and Thyme 3" class="attachment-700 size-700" height="233" sizes="(max-width: 350px) 100vw, 350px" src="https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-3-e1312149268371.jpg" srcset="https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-3-e1312149268371.jpg 350w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-3-e1312149268371-300x200.jpg 300w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-3-e1312149268371-205x135.jpg 205w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-3-e1312149268371-259x173.jpg 259w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-3-e1312149268371-150x100.jpg 150w" width="350"/></div>
</li>
<li class="wprm-recipe-instruction">
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-text">Meanwhile in a large frying pan, heat a tablespoon of oil on medium high heat. Cook bacon, stirring occasionally, until it gets crispy. Then remove the bacon to plate and set aside.<br/></div>
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-image"><img alt="Brussels Sprouts with Bacon and Thyme 4" class="attachment-700 size-700" height="183" sizes="(max-width: 550px) 100vw, 550px" src="https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-4-e1312149428617.jpg" srcset="https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-4-e1312149428617.jpg 550w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-4-e1312149428617-350x116.jpg 350w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-4-e1312149428617-150x50.jpg 150w" width="550"/></div>
</li>
<li class="wprm-recipe-instruction">
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-text">Heat the pan to medium high heat and add Brussels sprouts and thyme in the same pan. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes or until they brown.<br/></div>
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-image"><img alt="Brussels Sprouts with Bacon and Thyme 5" class="attachment-700 size-700" height="184" sizes="(max-width: 550px) 100vw, 550px" src="https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-5-e1312149454953.jpg" srcset="https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-5-e1312149454953.jpg 550w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-5-e1312149454953-350x117.jpg 350w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-5-e1312149454953-150x50.jpg 150w" width="550"/></div>
</li>
<li class="wprm-recipe-instruction">
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-text">Stir in the apple cider vinegar, (salt,) pepper, and bacon and serve warm.<br/></div>
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-image"><img alt="Brussels Sprouts with Bacon and Thyme 6" class="attachment-700 size-700" height="233" sizes="(max-width: 350px) 100vw, 350px" src="https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-6-e1312149657383.jpg" srcset="https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-6-e1312149657383.jpg 350w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-6-e1312149657383-300x200.jpg 300w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-6-e1312149657383-205x135.jpg 205w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-6-e1312149657383-259x173.jpg 259w, https://www.justonecookbook.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/Brussels-Sprouts-with-Bacon-and-Thyme-6-e1312149657383-150x100.jpg 150w" width="350"/></div>
</li>
</ol>
