<p>Friends, put everything you know about cauliflower steak behind you. We’re entering a new era: the era of the <em>CRISPY</em>, <em>COATED</em>, <em>BAKED</em> cauliflower steak!</p>
<p>Not only is this recipe incredibly flavorful, colorful, and satisfying, but it also <strong>requires</strong> <strong>just 10 basic ingredients</strong>! Let me show you how it’s done.</p>

<p>The base for this recipe is raw cashews blended into a meal with arrowroot starch. For spices we went for sea salt, garlic powder, smoked paprika, cayenne, and curry powder. And this combination coats the cauliflower “steaks” in the most beautiful, bright-orange way!</p>

<p>Before dipping our “steaks” in the crust mixture, we must steam the cauliflower until it’s just barely tender, but not quite cooked. This helps ensure the cauliflower is done in the center by the time it crisps on the outside.</p>

<p>After a quick dredge in an almond “buttermilk” mixture, it’s time to coat (and I mean COAT) your “steaks” in this gorgeous crust. Swoon!</p>
<p>Just a light drizzle of oil before baking, and we’re practically there!</p>
