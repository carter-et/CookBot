<p>In college I subsisted on cafe lunches to power me through writing papers and studying for tests. Panera was a frequent stop of mine since they had salads and soups and <em>SO much coffee</em>. One of my go-to’s was their black bean soup and I’ve had it in the back of my mind to recreate it ever since.</p>
<p>Let me show you how easy this<strong> 1-pot, 30-minute</strong> version is!</p>

<p>This recipe starts by sautéing onion and garlic until fragrant, then adding canned black beans, vegetable broth, and plenty of salt, pepper, and spices.</p>
<p>The last-minute addition of dark chocolate totally made this soup! It provides depth of flavor, balances the heat, and ties all of the spices together. Swoon!</p>
