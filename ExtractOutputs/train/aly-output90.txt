<p>This recipe requires just <strong>10 ingredients and 1 bowl to make</strong> and comes together in<strong> about 1 hour</strong>.</p>
<p>The reason I love this recipe so much is it’s like eating pizza but without all the bread and grains. That’s right, it’s <strong>grain-free</strong>!</p>

<p>It starts with socca batter infused with garlic and nutritional yeast for flavor (oregano is optional!). Baking powder provides a little rise, but not so much that the edges don’t get browned and a little crisp.</p>
<p>For toppings, we went with red onion, sun-dried tomatoes, <a href="https://minimalistbaker.com/5-minute-macadamia-cheese-vegan-crudite/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">vegan macadamia nut cheese</a>, and <a href="https://minimalistbaker.com/easy-vegan-pesto-5-minutes/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">vegan pesto</a>. But really, whatever you have on hand will work!</p>
