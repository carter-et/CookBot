One of my absolute favorites is <strong>Candied Sweet Potatoes</strong>, or we call them <strong>Daigaku Imo</strong> (大学芋).</p> <p>When I was in kindergarten, I remember I went to harvest Japanese sweet potatoes as part of the school excursion.
