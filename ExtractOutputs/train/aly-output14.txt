data-insertion="after" data-insertion-no-dbg="" data-selector=".ai-insert-2-23549316"></div> <h2>5 Tips to Make That Creamy Tofu Dressing for Shiraae</h2> <p>The tofu dressing is made of 5 ingredients: tofu, sesame seeds, miso, soy sauce, and sugar. And a bit of salt to taste.</p> <h3>1. Drain tofu</h3> <p>It’s tricky how much you should drain your tofu, but you can always make the adjustment each time.</p> <p>You do not want to dilute your dressing with excess water from the tofu. At the same time, you do not want to remove the water completely as moist tofu adds tender texture to the dish.</p> <p>To find the balance, I usually drain for 15-30 minutes. If I want to shorten to 15 minutes, I would put something heavy on top of the tofu to facilitate the draining process. If I have 30 minutes, I would not put any weight and let it drain naturally.</p> <h3>2. Toast the sesame seeds</h3> <p>Yes, most of the sesame seeds sold in packages are already toasted. However, you can toast it again in the frying pan to bring out the more fragrance.</p> <h3>3. Use white miso</h3> <p>For the creamy tofu dressing, I like to use White Miso (Shiro Miso 白味噌) because the color and sweet flavor complement the mixture.
