<p>Friends, behold one of the best things that’s ever come out of my kitchen: a savory, saucy, Black Bean Plantain Enchilada Bake! Just <strong>10 ingredients required</strong> for this incredibly flavorful, hearty, comforting dish. Let’s do this!</p>

<p>This isn’t your average enchilada bake, as it relies on<strong> roasted slices of plantains in place of tortillas</strong>, which not only add a naturally sweet balance but also keep this recipe both <strong>gluten- and grain-free</strong>!</p>
<p>Just check out those plantains roasted until golden brown and slightly caramelized – swoon!</p>

<p>The next layer is black beans simply spiced with cumin and sea salt. We went with canned to save time, but you could also make our <a href="https://minimalistbaker.com/easy-1-pot-black-beans/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">1-Pot Black Beans from Scratch</a> if you’d rather!</p>

<p>All that’s left is sauce, and we went with two: a combination of our <a href="https://minimalistbaker.com/easy-red-enchilada-sauce/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Easy Red Enchilada Sauce</a> and this <a href="https://minimalistbaker.com/vegan-mexican-cheese-three-ways/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Creamy Cashew Queso</a>.</p>
<p>The two together make this dish saucy, cheesy, smoky, and utterly delicious. Now, let’s layer.</p>
