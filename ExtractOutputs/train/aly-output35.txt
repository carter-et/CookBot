The round lid is slightly smaller than the diameter of the saucepan, and the lid floats on top of the simmering foods. Why do we use the otoshibuta instead of the regular lid that comes with the pot? Even distribution of heat</strong></h4> <p>Otoshibuta ensures that the heat is evenly distributed, allowing the ingredients to cook quickly and evenly as they absorb all the delicious flavors. The cooking liquid circulates towards the lid and coats the top of the ingredients, without being stirred with a spoon (which could possibly break them apart).</p> <h4><strong>2. Prevents ingredients from breaking apart</strong></h4> <p>Otoshibuta holds the ingredients in place so they don’t’ move around in the liquid and don’t break apart during the simmering process.</p> <h4><strong>3. Prevents evaporation</strong></h4> <p>Using the otoshibuta prevents evaporation, but if you’re cooking with very minimal cooking liquid, you can place a regular lid (with otoshibuta inside) to prevent evaporation 100%.</p> <h3><strong>Where to Buy Otoshibuta + Quick &amp; Easy Homemade Otoshibuta</strong></h3> <p><img alt="How To Make Otoshibuta" class="size-full wp-image-22924 alignleft nitro-lazy nitro-lazy-empty" data-pin-title="Simmered Sweet Potatoes with Lemon さつまいものレモン煮" height="200" id="MTU4MjM5MDc5NS43Mjg3" nitro-lazy-src="https://d3eh3svpl1busq.cloudfront.net/KQYMGOLIdXGmoAcyJsPOrQDKktgCbwtG/assets/static/source/rev-c031098/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/How-To-Make-Otoshibuta.jpg" nitro-lazy-srcset="https://d3eh3svpl1busq.cloudfront.net/KQYMGOLIdXGmoAcyJsPOrQDKktgCbwtG/assets/static/source/rev-c031098/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/How-To-Make-Otoshibuta.jpg 200w, https://d3eh3svpl1busq.cloudfront.net/KQYMGOLIdXGmoAcyJsPOrQDKktgCbwtG/assets/static/source/rev-c031098/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/How-To-Make-Otoshibuta-150x150.jpg 150w, https://d3eh3svpl1busq.cloudfront.net/KQYMGOLIdXGmoAcyJsPOrQDKktgCbwtG/assets/static/source/rev-c031098/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/How-To-Make-Otoshibuta-182x182.jpg 182w, https://d3eh3svpl1busq.cloudfront.net/KQYMGOLIdXGmoAcyJsPOrQDKktgCbwtG/assets/static/source/rev-c031098/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/How-To-Make-Otoshibuta-115x115.jpg 115w" nitro-og-height="200" nitro-og-width="200" sizes="(max-width: 200px) 100vw, 200px" width="200"/>You can get the wooden otoshibuta (fixed width) on <a href="https://amzn.to/2DWKWQe" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Amazon</a> or stainless steel otoshibuta (adjustable size) from <a href="https://www.mtckitchen.com/adjustable-stainless-steel-drop-lid/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">this online shop</a>.</p> <p>Or you can easily make an otoshibuta with a sheet of aluminum foil or parchment paper.
