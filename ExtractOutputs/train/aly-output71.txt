<h3>How to Make a Burrito Bowl</h3>
<p>You can make a burrito bowl literally however you want. But this is how we build ours:</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Grain</strong>: <a href="https://minimalistbaker.com/how-to-cook-white-rice/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">White rice</a>, <a href="https://minimalistbaker.com/how-to-cook-brown-rice-2-ways/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">brown rice</a>, or <a href="https://minimalistbaker.com/how-to-make-cauliflower-rice/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">cauliflower rice</a> (<a href="https://minimalistbaker.com/how-to-make-green-rice/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Green Rice</a> would be amazing, too!)</li>
<li><strong>Protein</strong>: <a href="https://minimalistbaker.com/1-pan-mexican-shredded-chicken/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Shredded Mexican Chicken</a> (omit and double up on beans if vegan / vegetarian)</li>
<li><strong>Legume</strong>: <a href="https://minimalistbaker.com/easy-1-pot-black-beans/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Black beans</a> (or, let’s be real, canned) simmered with sea salt and spices and slightly smashed for a “refried bean” effect</li>
<li><strong>Veggies</strong>: Stir-fried peppers and onions + roasted sweet potatoes</li>
<li><strong>Sauce</strong>: <a href="https://minimalistbaker.com/easy-red-salsa/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Fresh salsa</a> (or, let’s be real, store-bought)</li>
<li><strong>Guac</strong>: <a href="https://minimalistbaker.com/baked-plantain-chips-garlicky-guacamole/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Our favorite garlicky guacamole</a></li>
<li><strong>Garnish</strong>: Fresh cilantro</li>
</ol>
<p>Bam. Dinner’s on the table <em>fast</em>.</p>
