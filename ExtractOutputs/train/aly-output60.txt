<p>We recently realized that our <em>favorite</em> pasta salad had only been featured in <a aria-label="a video (opens in a new tab)" href="https://minimalistbaker.com/how-to-roast-vegetables/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">a video</a> and not as a recipe on the blog. That obviously needed to change. The people need this recipe.</p>
<p>Not only is it delicious, it’s also <strong>gluten-free</strong> and <strong>vegan</strong> and comes together in just <strong>30 minutes</strong> with <strong>10 ingredients</strong>! </p>

<span id="more-49414"></span>
<p>It starts with roasting broccoli with olive oil, salt, and pepper until browned and tender.</p>
<p>Then comes cooking the pasta. We like to use Trader Joe’s Gluten-Free Brown Rice Quinoa Fusili as we’ve found it has the best taste and texture without compromising quality of ingredients.</p>

<p>The pesto is made in a <a aria-label="food processor (opens in a new tab)" href="https://aax-us-east.amazon-adsystem.com/x/c/Qj92WMVvfXvLYUG_NEDk7ToAAAFurf24BwEAAAFKAelh6rI/https://www.amazon.com/dp/B00LBZOYAK/?creativeASIN=B00LBZOYAK&amp;linkCode=w61&amp;imprToken=qGUN0MLXfnrJrW2LeAMlYg&amp;slotNum=7&amp;tag=minimalistbaker-20" rel="noreferrer noopener nofollow" target="_blank">food processor</a> by mixing basil, garlic, hemp seeds, lemon juice, nutritional yeast, and sea salt, then drizzling in olive oil to achieve a creamy paste. Excuse us while we jump in for a swim.</p>

<p>Finally, it’s time to assemble! Add your roasted broccoli, cooked pasta, and fresh pesto to a bowl along with sun-dried tomatoes and <a aria-label="macadamia nut cheese (opens in a new tab)" href="https://minimalistbaker.com/5-minute-macadamia-cheese-vegan-crudite/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">macadamia nut cheese</a> (optional). Major swoon.</p>

<ol class="wprm-recipe-instructions">
<li class="wprm-recipe-instruction">
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-text"><p>Heat oven to 400 degrees F (204 C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Add broccoli and toss with oil, salt, and pepper. Once oven is preheated, add broccoli and cook for 15 minutes or until lightly golden browned.</p></div>
</li>
<li class="wprm-recipe-instruction">
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-text"><p>In the meantime, bring a small pot of water to a boil and add salt (to season the water and the pasta). Once boiling, add the pasta, stir, and cook according to package instructions or until al dente. Drain and set aside.</p></div>
</li>
<li class="wprm-recipe-instruction">
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-text"><p>In the meantime, prepare pesto. Add basil, garlic, hemp seeds, lemon juice, nutritional yeast, and sea salt to a <a href="https://www.amazon.com/dp/B00LBZOYAK/?creativeASIN=B00LBZOYAK&amp;linkCode=w61&amp;imprToken=UBrLJzl2MywOrjpFp-V66w&amp;slotNum=6&amp;tag=minimalistbaker-20" rel="nofollow" target="_blank">food processor</a> and mix to combine. Then stream in olive oil a little at a time until a creamy paste is achieved. Scrape down sides as needed. Taste and adjust flavor as needed, adding more salt to taste, nutritional yeast for cheesiness, hemp seeds or basil to thicken, garlic for kick, or lemon juice for acidity. Cover with lid and set aside.</p></div>
</li>
<li class="wprm-recipe-instruction">
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-text"><p>Once broccoli is roasted, cool slightly then add to a large serving / mixing bowl. Then add cooked drained pasta, sun-dried tomatoes, little spoonfuls of macadamia nut cheese (optional), and red pepper flake (optional). Top with pesto and toss gently to combine (being careful to not break the pasta).</p></div>
</li>
<li class="wprm-recipe-instruction">
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-text">Enjoy immediately or refrigerate up to 3-4 days (not freezer friendly). Enjoy chilled or at room temperature.</div>
</li>
</ol>
</div>
