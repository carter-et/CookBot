<p>Say hello to our new favorite salad.</p>
<p>Believe it or not, it was inspired by one we tried in an airport! Of all the places to glean inspiration from…</p>
<p>But no matter the source, the end result is <em>far</em> more delicious than the original we tried (not to brag, but for real), <em>and</em> it’s insanely easy to make! Shall we?</p>

<p>This <strong>30-minute recipe</strong> starts with a base of greens, cooked lentils, red grapes, and the star of the show: curry roasted cauliflower.</p>
<p>We tossed one head of cauliflower in a bit of salt, coconut oil (or water), and our <a href="https://minimalistbaker.com/diy-curry-powder/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">DIY Curry Powder</a> (swoon) and roasted it to perfection. The result is crispy-on-the-edge, tender-in-the-center cauliflower bursting with flavor.</p>

<p>Next comes the dressing, which is a mixture of our go-to <a href="https://minimalistbaker.com/easy-green-curry-paste/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Green Curry Paste</a> – that color tho! – and tahini, resulting in a creamy, savory-sweet, punchy dressing that marries this salad together beautifully.</p>

<h2 class="wprm-recipe-name">Curried Cauliflower, Grape &amp; Lentil Salad</h2>
<div class="wprm-recipe-summary">
		Crazy delicious kale salad topped with red grapes, lentils, and curry roasted cauliflower. Served with a tahini-green curry paste dressing! Just 30 minutes required for this flavorful side or entrée.	</div>

<div class="wprm-recipe-instructions-container">
<h3 class="wprm-recipe-header">Instructions</h3>
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-group">
<ol class="wprm-recipe-instructions">
<li class="wprm-recipe-instruction">
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-text"><p>Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (204 C). Line a baking sheet (or more as needed) with parchment paper.</p></div>
</li>
<li class="wprm-recipe-instruction">
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-text"><p>Add cauliflower to a mixing bowl and toss with coconut oil (or water), curry powder, and sea salt. Transfer to baking sheet and roast cauliflower for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown and tender.</p></div>
</li>
<li class="wprm-recipe-instruction">
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-text">Prepare dressing by adding green curry paste, tahini, lemon juice, maple syrup, salt, and pepper to a mixing bowl and whisking to combine. If needed, thin with water until pourable.</div>
</li>
<li class="wprm-recipe-instruction">
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-text">Taste and adjust flavor as needed, adding more green curry paste for a stronger curry flavor, tahini for greater thickness, lemon juice for acidity, or maple syrup for sweetness.</div>
</li>
<li class="wprm-recipe-instruction">
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-text">Assemble salad by adding lettuce to a serving platter or bowl. Top with lentils, grapes, and cooked cauliflower and serve with dressing. Optional: garnish with fresh cilantro.</div>
</li>
<li class="wprm-recipe-instruction">
<div class="wprm-recipe-instruction-text">Best served fresh. Store leftovers in airtight container for 3-4 days. Store dressing separately for up to 1 week.</div>
</li>
</ol>
