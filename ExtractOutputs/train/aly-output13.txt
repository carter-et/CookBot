Read on for tips &amp; tricks on how to make the perfect fried rice at home.</p> <p><img alt="Rice bowls containing shrimp fried rice." class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-91763 nitro-lazy nitro-lazy-empty" data-pin-description="Who needs take-out when you can make this quick &amp; tasty wok charred shrimp fried rice at home! #shrimp #friedrice #chinesefood | Easy Japanese Recipes at JustOneCookbook.com" data-pin-title="Shrimp Fried Rice 海老チャーハン" height="1200" nitro-lazy-src="https://d3eh3svpl1busq.cloudfront.net/KQYMGOLIdXGmoAcyJsPOrQDKktgCbwtG/assets/static/optimized/rev-c031098/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Shrimp-Fried-Rice-6485-III.jpg" nitro-og-height="1200" nitro-og-width="800" width="800"/></p> <h2>Tricks and Tips to Make the Perfect Fried Rice at Home</h2> <h4><strong>1. Wok</strong></h4> <p>You need a large wok or frying pan that has enough space for the rice to move around. The key is to make sure each rice grain comes in contact with the oil and heat. Give the rice and all the ingredients some good tossing. To prevent big clumps, do not overcrowd the ingredients. And cook 1-2 servings at a time.</p> <h4><strong>2. Heat</strong></h4> <p>Fried rice at the restaurant tastes good because it has that smoky flavor from the high temperature and the seasoned wok. Cooking over high heat is required and a layer of oil on a hot surface will form a non-stick coating on the wok. If the wok is not hot enough, rice will start sticking everywhere. Since you will be cooking on high heat, make sure all the ingredients are prepped and ready to throw in. If you decide to use a non-stick frying pan, don’t use high heat as you will ruin your pan.</p> <h4><strong>3. Oil</strong></h4> <p>You will need a decent amount of oil to cook the fluffy egg. I know you might prefer to use as little oil as possible. But you don’t want your fried rice to stick on your pan and ruin your dinner right? Don’t worry about oily egg because you would be adding the egg back into the wok later on. The oil on the egg will coat the rice and help rice separate from each other.</p> <h4><strong>4. Rice</strong></h4> <p>You can’t make fried rice with freshly cooked rice. Use day-old cold rice because it is more firm and has no excess moisture. Again, we want the rice to separate and not get mushy. If you don’t have leftover rice in hand and still wish to make fried rice. Then cook the rice ahead and let cool in the refrigerator for at least a few hours to reduce the moisture content.</p> <h4><strong>5. Ingredients &amp; Seasonings</strong></h4> <p>Use ingredients that do not contain too much moisture. Extra moisture or too much saucy seasonings will turn the fried rice mushy. Other than that, be creative and use any leftover meats and veggies you find in the fridge (but keep it simple)!</p> <p><img alt="Rice bowls containing shrimp fried rice." class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-91764 nitro-lazy nitro-lazy-empty" data-pin-description="Who needs take-out when you can make this quick &amp; tasty wok charred shrimp fried rice at home! #shrimp #friedrice #chinesefood | Easy Japanese Recipes at JustOneCookbook.com" data-pin-title="Shrimp Fried Rice 海老チャーハン" height="533" id="MTU4MjM5MDMwOC4zNzg3" nitro-lazy-src="https://d3eh3svpl1busq.cloudfront.net/KQYMGOLIdXGmoAcyJsPOrQDKktgCbwtG/assets/static/optimized/rev-c031098/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Shrimp-Fried-Rice-6496-I.jpg" nitro-lazy-srcset="https://d3eh3svpl1busq.cloudfront.net/KQYMGOLIdXGmoAcyJsPOrQDKktgCbwtG/assets/static/optimized/rev-c031098/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Shrimp-Fried-Rice-6496-I.jpg 800w, https://d3eh3svpl1busq.cloudfront.net/KQYMGOLIdXGmoAcyJsPOrQDKktgCbwtG/assets/static/optimized/rev-c031098/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/Shrimp-Fried-Rice-6496-I-300x200.jpg 300w" nitro-og-height="533" nitro-og-width="800" sizes="(max-width: 800px) 100vw, 800px" width="800"/></p> <h2>Fresh Cooked Fried Rice for Family Weeknight Meals</h2> <p>When making this <strong>shrimp fried rice</strong> at home for the family, you get to use better quality oil, ingredients, and less salt.
