<p>For years I’ve been wanting to make a plant-based take on palak paneer. The vibrant green color lures me in every time. Thankfully, this recipe is not only simple to make, but also incredibly delicious and requires just <strong>30 minutes</strong>! Let me show you how it’s done.</p>

<p>Next you’ll sauté onion, garlic, ginger, and serrano pepper with cumin seed. Once browned, you’ll add diced tomatoes and cook until tender.</p>

This creates a creamy base for the palak paneer, which will blend beautifully with your cheesy, crispy tofu!</p>
