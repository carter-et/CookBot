chiffon cake is to make perfect meringue and fold it into batter correctly.</p> <h3>Meringue</h3> <p>In Japan, the majority of recipes call for cold egg whites to make meringue (and we don’t use cream of tartar).  If you already know how to make meringue with room temperature egg whites and cream of tartar, please use your own method.  Otherwise, you can follow my detailed instructions in the recipe.</p> <h3>Fold In</h3> <p>When you finish making meringue, make sure to fold in egg whites gently so the tiny bubbles don’t deflate.  If you deflate the egg whites too much, the cake will not be fluffy.</p> <p><img alt="Earl Grey Chiffon Cake served on a cake stand." class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-92744 nitro-lazy nitro-lazy-empty" data-pin-description="Light, spongy and airy with exceptionally light and airy that I feel like I can gobble up half of the cake myself.</p> <p>It is also slightly healthier than other types of baked goods that use plenty of butter.  This earl grey
